EasyUnsub is one of a few apps created by GroupSRC. We've also created two other apps that make Reddit a little easier to use.

rDrafts

rDrafts is our most popular app. It allows you to create a draft of a Reddit post, preview it, share the preview with others, and then submit it at your convenience (no scheduling though).

Visit rdrafts.gropusrc.com

EasyAMA

EasyAMA is similar to rDrafts, except that it is specifically designed for AMAs. We created this app to give users a template for creating a successful AMA. The app walks the user through the AMA planning process and encourages them to submit an AMA that is informative, valuable, and verified.

Visit easyama.groupsrc.com

About GroupSRC

GroupSRC is a couple of brothers that are interested in not only Reddit, but online communities in general. We believe in the power of online communities and want to develop the communities and tools that allow people to do amazing things together.

The other side of this equation is getting more leaders and businesses involved. We think there is an ethical and valuable way for leaders/businesses to engage Reddit and other online commuities and we try to help them do just that. We even have an entire blog dedicated to "Reddit Marketing" that everyone seems to hate - MarketersGuideToReddit.com
